#####Suzlon-Sustainability-Project This project was developed to demonstrate how Data Analytics can drive Sustainability and Operational Excellence in a large-scale corporate estate. Using SQL, I analyzed energy consumption patterns to identify grid-dependency and performed gap analysis on space utilization to optimize desk allocation.

**Results & Business Insights **
Sustainability Scorecard:

Successfully tracked the energy mix across the campus, showing that 82% of total energy consumption was met through renewable sources (Wind & Solar).

Identified a 12% reduction in Grid-dependency over a 6-month trend, resulting in significant utility cost savings.

Space Optimization (Gap Analysis):

Analyzed desk occupancy across departments. Found that the Finance and Operations sectors reached 94% occupancy, triggering a recommendation for space refurbishment or desk-sharing models.

Identified 15% under-utilized space in the Annex buildings, providing an opportunity for team consolidation to save on HVAC and lighting costs.

Cost-Efficiency Impact:

By correlating energy peaks with building occupancy, the analysis suggested a "Smart Lighting" schedule that could potentially reduce monthly operational expenses by ₹1.2 Lakhs.

Management Readiness:

Developed a series of Executive Dashboards and Canva Infographics that translate these technical data points into "Ready-to-Present" slides for leadership reviews.
